Scholarly Notes

This app is based on the BORI Critical Edition of the Mahabharata, considered the most authoritative scholarly version.

All translations are fact-checked against multiple sources and peer-reviewed scholarship.

Help & Guide

How to Use This App

  • Search Verses: Enter any word, phrase, or concept to find relevant verses
  • Character Search: Search for specific characters to find all related verses
  • Episode Search: Find verses related to specific episodes or events
  • Theme Search: Explore verses by thematic content (dharma, karma, etc.)
  • Advanced Search: Use filters to narrow down results by parva, character, or verse range

Language Support

  • English: Modern scholarly translations
  • Hindi: Devanagari script with Roman transliteration
  • Sanskrit: Original text in Devanagari with Roman transliteration

Scholarly Sources

All content is based on:

  • Ganguli's Translation (Reference)
  • Van Buitenen's Translation (Reference)
